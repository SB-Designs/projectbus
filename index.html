Who we are

We're two young web developers, Sebastian and lovine868, both with a passion for buses. The bus-loving community is a big one, and we want to contribute to it, building bus-related projects that bring you joy. We began in 2026, with the start of development for Chelmsford Bus Simulator. As we progress, we aim to build and release more bus projects, from apps to games.

Chelmsford Bus Simulator

Explore the county of Essex with a variety of buses and routes, with a to-scale network of Chelmsford's road systems, and realistic models of the buses that serve.
